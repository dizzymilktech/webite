“the flood”

A simple choice,
To open the valve,
and let the flood pour through,
I knew nothing.

A noble choice,
To let the water sweep me away,
and let my love fizzle out,
They knew nothing.

A choice of despair,
To glide where it took us,
At the cost of autonomy,
We knew nothing.

A choice of grief,
To drown for a world who never cared,
and choke for the innocence wronged,
You knew nothing.
